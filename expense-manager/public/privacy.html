Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2, 2026

Overview

ExpenseIQ is a personal finance management application. Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains how we handle your data.

Data Storage

ExpenseIQ stores all financial data locally on your device using your browser's IndexedDB storage. No financial data is sent to or stored on any external server.

Google Sign-In

If you choose to sign in with Google (optional), we access:

  • Basic profile information (name, email, profile picture) — used only to display your identity in the app
  • Google Drive App Data (drive.appdata scope) — used only to store encrypted backups of your financial data in a hidden app-specific folder on your Google Drive that only ExpenseIQ can access

We do not access, read, or modify any other files on your Google Drive.

Data We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not collect analytics or usage data
  • We do not use cookies for tracking
  • We do not share any data with third parties
  • We do not have access to your financial data — it stays on your device

Cloud Backup

Cloud backup to Google Drive is entirely optional. When enabled, your data is stored in Google Drive's app-specific data folder, which is not visible or accessible to other applications.

Data Deletion

You can delete all your data at any time by clearing your browser's site data or using the app's settings. Cloud backups can be removed from your Google Drive settings.

Open Source

ExpenseIQ is open source. You can review the complete source code at github.com/vikasreddykamalapuram/expense-manager.
